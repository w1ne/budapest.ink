 ### All Stars Festival Preparty | 3 rooms | Bachata - Kizomba - Salsa

The All Stars Festival Preparty is an exciting event taking place on Thursday, November 2, 2023, from 1:15 PM to 8:00 PM UTC+01. The party will be held in Budapest, Hungary, at the Aquincum Hotel, located at Árpád fejedelem útja 94.

This preparty is a lead-up to the All Stars Festival and will feature three rooms dedicated to different dance styles: Bachata, Kizomba, and Salsa. The event will bring together international instructors, DJs, and guest dancers to create a vibrant and energetic atmosphere.

The program will start at 1:15 PM with the opening of the doors. From 1:30 PM to 10:00 PM, there will be party workshops where participants can learn and improve their dance skills. In the Salsa room, Mo T from Hungary will teach Salsa On2 Body Isolation and Partnerwork, followed by Laci & Sugi from DameUna in Hungary, who will lead a Rueda de Casino workshop suitable for all skill levels.

In the Bachata room, Imi & Rita from Hungary will teach Sensual Bachata without step for intermediate level dancers, and Marko & Anita from Croatia will lead a Bachata Fusion workshop for the same level of dancers. In the Urban Kiz room, Eddy & Anita from Hungary will teach MusiKiz for intermediate level dancers, and Alvin & Raluca from the UK will offer an Urban Kiz Progressive workshop.

After the workshops, the party will kick off at 10:00 PM and continue until 4:00 AM, featuring three rooms dedicated to Bachata, Salsa, and Kizomba/Urban Kiz. The lineup of DJs includes DJ Balito, DJ Shisha, DJ Brumi, and DJ Dennis the Chemist for Bachata, DJ Dennis the Chemist and DJ Missy for Salsa, and DJ Azimbo, DJ Azmael, and DJ Remcy for Kizomba/Urban Kiz.

The dress code for the event is "RED KISS." The organizers kindly request attendees to dress accordingly. The preparty can be attended by purchasing a ticket which includes both the workshops and the party for 6000 Ft (Hungarian Forint), or a party-only ticket for 4000 Ft. It's important to note that the preparty is not included in any festival pass, so separate tickets need to be purchased.

For those who prefer to use their SZÉP card (a type of Hungarian payment card), it can be used for online purchases. However, it's worth noting that there will be a bar at the venue that operates with a card-only payment policy, so attendees should come prepared to make purchases using their bank cards.

Overall, the All Stars Festival Preparty promises to be a fantastic event, bringing together dance enthusiasts and professionals for an unforgettable night of music, workshops, and performances. Don't miss the opportunity to be part of this incredible event in Budapest. For more information and to purchase tickets, please visit the All Stars Festival website at www.allstarsfestival.com or contact info@allstarsfestival.com.

 ### EVENT IS FULL - Mexican Documentary Screening and Taco Evening

EVENT IS FULL - Mexican Documentary Screening and Taco Evening

Join us for a vibrant and immersive Mexican-themed night on Friday, November 3, 2023, at 9:30 AM UTC+01, following the joyous celebration of Dia de los Muertos. Hosted by Mira Intercultural Community Hungary, this event combines cultural appreciation, documentary screening, and delicious Mexican tacos.

The highlight of the evening is the documentary screening of "Beyond the Desert" by director Ana Zayas and biologist Omar Calva. This captivating documentary explores the cave fauna and emphasizes the importance of preserving their habitat in the Sonoran Desert, Mexico. The film takes the viewers on an adventure through three caves, showcasing Omar's faunistic studies conducted during his master's degree at the University of Sonora. The documentary lasts for 20 minutes and will be followed by an introduction and discussion with Omar, the director.

After the screening, indulge your taste buds in a delightful taco-tasting experience. We will be serving authentic Mexican beef tacos, which are known for their rich and savory flavors. In addition to the classic beef tacos, we also offer marinated soy tacos as an equally scrumptious vegetarian option. Please note that traditional Mexican cuisine isn't inherently spicy, but we will provide an assortment of sauces on the side, allowing you to customize the spice level according to your preference.

The event will take place at Mira Ház, Lujza utca 36, Budapest. It will begin at 5:30 PM and end at 8:00 PM. Registration is required as we can accommodate a maximum of 15 people during this event. Please make sure to register quickly to secure your spot by visiting the following link: [registration link]

Come one, come all, and let's celebrate the richness of Mexican culture, the wonders of nature, and the joy of community. Don't miss this opportunity to immerse yourself in the magic of Mexico. We look forward to seeing you at the documentary screening and taco evening!
