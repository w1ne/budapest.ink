 ### SPEEDRUN w/ umru, 99jakes, Lil Kevo 303

Join us for the SPEEDRUN event featuring umru, 99jakes, and Lil Kevo 303! This electrifying night of music will showcase the diverse talents of these artists as they bring their unique sounds to the stage.

umru is a Brooklyn-based Estonian-American producer who has quickly made a name for himself in the pop music scene. With collaborations with artists like Charli XCX and A. G. Cook, umru's music seamlessly blends intense, bass-heavy sound design with catchy melodies. His work has helped define the emerging space between underground and pop music, and he continues to push the boundaries of music production.

If you're a fan of hardcore and happy hardcore music, then you won't want to miss 99jakes. As a co-founder of the hardcore collective and label 909 worldwide, she is known for her aggressive basslines, dizzying stab-lines, and uplifting melodies. Get ready for a high-energy set that will keep you dancing all night long.

Lil Kevo 303, representing 909 Worldwide's East Coast Division, brings a unique blend of distorted white noise and pitch-shifting breakbeats to the table. His music is characterized by delicate melodies, complex rhythms, and intriguing soundscapes that take listeners on a journey. Prepare to be captivated by his emotionally charged tracks.

The event will take place at Toldi Klub in Budapest on November 2, 2023, from 22:00 to 05:00. Tickets are priced at 4.500ft for the second round and can be purchased at the door for 5.000ft. Note that the first round of tickets has already sold out.

Please note that SPEEDRUN has a zero-tolerance policy for sexism, racism, homophobia, transphobia, discrimination, or any form of disrespectful behavior. Let's create a safe and inclusive space for everyone to enjoy the music.

 ### All Stars Festival Preparty | 3 rooms | Bachata - Kizomba - Salsa

Join us for the All Stars Festival Preparty on Thursday, November 2, 2023, from 1:15 PM to 8:00 PM UTC+01. This exciting event will take place in Budapest, Hungary, at the Aquincum Hotel.

The All Stars Festival Preparty will feature three dance rooms dedicated to Bachata, Kizomba, and Salsa. With international instructors, DJs, and guest dancers, this preparty is a must-attend for dance enthusiasts.

The program kicks off at 1:15 PM with the opening of the doors. From 1:30 PM to 10:00 PM, there will be various workshop sessions where you can learn and improve your skills in Salsa, Bachata, and Urban Kizomba. The workshops will cover different levels, from beginners to intermediate, so there is something for everyone.

Here is a breakdown of the workshop schedule:

Salsa:

- 1:30 PM - 2:15 PM: Mo T (Hungary) - Salsa On2 Body Isolation & Partnerwork
- 2:15 PM - 3:00 PM: Laci & Sugi (DameUna / Hungary) - Rueda de Casino (All Levels)

Bachata:

- 1:30 PM - 2:15 PM: Imi & Rita (Hungary) - Sensual Bachata without Step (Intermediate)
- 2:15 PM - 3:00 PM: Marko & Anita (Croatia) - Bachata Fusion (Intermediate)

Urban Kizomba:

- 1:30 PM - 2:15 PM: Eddy & Anita (Hungary) - MusiKiz (Intermediate)
- 2:15 PM - 3:00 PM: Alvin & Raluca (UK) - Urban Kiz Progressive Workshop

After the workshops, the party will begin at 10:00 PM and continue until 4:00 AM in the three dance rooms dedicated to Bachata, Salsa, and Kizomba/Urban Kiz. Get ready to dance the night away to the beats of DJ Balito, DJ Shisha, DJ Brumi, DJ Dennis the Chemist (for Bachata), DJ Dennis the Chemist and DJ Missy (for Salsa), and DJ Azimbo, DJ Azmael, and DJ Remcy (for Kizomba/Urban Kiz).

The dress code for the preparty is "Red Kiss," so come dressed in your finest red attire to add to the vibrant atmosphere of the event.

The entry fee for the All Stars Festival Preparty is 6000 Ft (approximately 15 euros) for workshops and the party or 4000 Ft (approximately 10 euros) for the party only. Please note that the preparty is not included in any festival pass, so make sure to purchase your ticket separately. Payments can be made on-site in cash, by card at the venue, or online with SZÉP card.

Bar services will be available at the venue, but please note that payment can only be made by card. The Aquincum Hotel, located at Budapest 1036, Árpád fejedelem útja 94, will be hosting this exciting preparty event.

Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to kickstart your All Stars Festival experience. Join Balito, Viola, Timi, and the All Stars Festival team for a night of dance, music, and unforgettable memories.

For more information about the All Stars Festival Preparty, visit the official website at www.allstarsfestival.com or contact the organizers at info@allstarsfestival.com.

 ### EVENT IS FULL - Mexican Documentary Screening and Taco Evening

**EVENT IS FULL - Mexican Documentary Screening and Taco Evening**

Join us for a vibrant and immersive Mexican-themed night on November 3rd, following the joyous celebration of Dia de los Muertos. The event is organized by Mira Intercultural Community Hungary and promises to be an exciting experience that celebrates Mexican culture.

The highlight of the evening will be a documentary screening of "Beyond the Desert" by director Ana Zayas and biologist Omar Calva. This documentary, which blends adventure and science, focuses on the cave fauna and highlights the importance of their habitat in the Sonoran Desert, Mexico. Omar, the protagonist of the film, will be present to introduce the documentary and discuss his faunistic studies and the conservation efforts needed to protect these species. The documentary is approximately 20 minutes long.

After the screening, attendees will have the opportunity to indulge in a delightful taco-tasting experience. The event will feature the most beloved Mexican delicacy - beef tacos. In addition to the classic beef option, there will also be marinated soy tacos available for those looking for a vegetarian alternative. The organizers will provide an assortment of sauces on the side, allowing each participant to customize the spice level of their tacos according to their preference.

The event will take place at Mira Ház, located at Lujza utca 36, Budapest. The event will start at 17:30 and conclude at 20:00. Please note that registration is required, and although the event is free of charge, only 15 people can be accommodated. To secure your spot, please register using the following link: [Registration Form](https://forms.gle/P9csn2N48eHaRFzc7). 

Don't miss this opportunity to celebrate the richness of Mexican culture, explore the wonders of nature, and enjoy the company of like-minded individuals. Come and let's taco 'bout the magic of Mexico together at this incredible event!
