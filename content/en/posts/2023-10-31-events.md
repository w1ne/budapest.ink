 ### International Halloween Madness by ELB ✘ Akvárium ✘ 31st Oct

Are you ready for the biggest party of the year? ELB presents International Halloween Madness at Akvárium on October 31st. This is set to be the craziest Halloween party ever, brought to you by Erasmus Life Budapest. Get ready for a night of fun, music, and costumes!

There are some important updates for the event. 300 tickets will be available at the door for 7000 Ft, so make sure to arrive early as doors open at 11:00 PM. If you want to pay at the door, remember to bring cash. The party will continue until 6:00 AM.

Regular tickets have already sold out online, but there are still VIP Last Minute tickets available. These tickets come with perks such as fast-lane entrance, exclusive VIP terrace and area, and a great view of the stage. If you’re looking for a different Halloween experience, Terminal 2000's Halloween Madness tickets are also available. These tickets grant access to Akvárium after 3:00 AM.

It's important to note that if you have a pre-sale ticket, you'll need to arrive before 1:00 AM to ensure entry without waiting for others to leave. Don't forget to bring your ticket QR code ID card and ELB Card with you.

The event will take place at Akvárium Klub, which has a capacity of 2500 people across 3 dance floors. Located in the heart of Budapest, it's the perfect venue for this international Halloween party. Join partygoers from all over the world on October 31st, which happens to be a public holiday.

The lineup for the night includes the best DJs in Budapest. In the Big Hall, you can enjoy EDM, mainstream hits, and club house music by Rusty and Palo. In the Small Hall, DJs Matt Flaron and Thomas Rush will be playing R'n'B, hip-hop, reggaeton, and Latin music. The Lokál room will feature DJs Franek303 and Schaeder from Germany, as well as Rovizz and Kloos playing tech house and techno.

Prepare to be amazed by the craziest Halloween decorations in Budapest, and don't forget to wear your best costume! There will be a special show, a party photo booth, and a best costume competition with exciting prizes.

Tickets for the event can be purchased online or at the Erasmus Life Budapest front office. Prices range from 10-17.5€, depending on the ticket category. If you have an ELB Card, you can enjoy discounted prices. For those looking to save on their costume, Maszka costume rental offers a 20% discount if you mention "Halloween Madness" or show your ELB Card.

Don't miss out on this incredible Halloween party. Get your tickets now and join the international community in Budapest for a night of spooky fun and unforgettable memories. And if you haven't already, sign up for an ELB Card to enjoy discounts on gyms, restaurants, bars, and access to international trips, parties, and activities.

Join the other ELB events too: parties, trips, tours, and activities! Check out the Erasmus Life Budapest Cooltix page for more information.
