## Explore Medieval History at Buda Castle Walking Tour

Join us for an immersive journey through time on The Medieval History Tour of the Buda Castle. Delve into the captivating past of Budapest as you walk through the historic UNESCO World Heritage site with our expert guides. 

Discover the secrets of medieval Hungary as you learn about tax collection practices, the bustling marketplaces, and the architectural marvels of the era. Capture breathtaking views from Buda Castle, witness the remnants of medieval structures, and explore hidden corners and courtyards filled with stories waiting to be told.

Listen to tales of Tamas Bakocz, the archbishop of Esztergom, and his role in rallying the people of Hungary for the crusades against the Ottomans. Peek into mysterious cellars and caves, uncover the UNESCO World Heritage designation's significance, and uncover intriguing historical tidbits like the one-time dog market in Hungary.

Led by licensed native Hungarian guides Sabi or Attila, this English-language tour promises an engaging 2 to 2.5-hour experience filled with knowledge, anecdotes, and surprises. Meet us at the designated meeting point on Becsi Kapu next to Vienna Gate, where you'll spot our guides holding yellow umbrellas with our company logo. 

Whether you arrive by Bus 16 or opt for a scenic 12-minute walk from Szell Kalmen Metro Station, you're in for a treat. Our small group sizes ensure a personalized experience with ample opportunities to interact, ask questions, and enjoy a dynamic tour of the Buda Castle, a place steeped in history and culture.

Don't miss this chance to explore where Dracula was once held captive and why certain shop signs are protected in Hungary. Join us on Saturdays, Sundays, and Mondays to embark on a memorable journey through the medieval past of Budapest.
