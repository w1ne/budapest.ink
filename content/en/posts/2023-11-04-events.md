The venue for this special celebration is called Enter Bp, located at Duna-lejáró utca, 1211 in Budapest. The event will kick off at 1:00 PM and continue until 9:00 PM, giving you plenty of time to groove to some amazing tunes.

Whether you're a long-time fan of Dubapest HiFi or you're just looking for a memorable night out, this is an event you won't want to miss. So mark your calendars, grab your dancing shoes, and get ready to celebrate Dubapest HiFi's birthday in style at the Budapest Dub Club►Dubapest Birthday Bash on November 4, 2023. See you there!
