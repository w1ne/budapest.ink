 ### First Came The Shadow [FR] @SzimplaROCKS

Title: First Came The Shadow [FR] @SzimplaROCKS: A Captivating Post-Rock Experience at Szimpla Kert

Date: To be announced
Time: To be announced
Location: Szimpla Kert, Budapest
Organizer: Szimpla Kert

Join us for an extraordinary evening of post-rock music as First Came The Shadow takes the stage at Szimpla Kert. This captivating quartet hailing from Nice, France, will mesmerize audiences with their richly textured harmonies and wide soundscapes that effortlessly blend wonder, rage, and melancholy.

Formed in 2017, First Came The Shadow has quickly gained recognition for their unique approach to post-rock. With their captivating performances, they transport listeners to a world where emotions and melodies intertwine, leaving a lasting impression.

In May 2018, First Came The Shadow released their first EP titled "Premonition," which showcased their talent for creating intricate and evocative musical arrangements. Following the release, the band embarked on numerous tours across France, as well as Spain and Portugal, cultivating a dedicated fanbase along the way.

In March 2020, the band released their eagerly awaited album, "Aridity." This album delves into the fateful collapse of the post-modern world, exploring the impact of mankind's actions and the consequences they bring. Through their music, First Came The Shadow invites listeners to reflect on the complexities of our society and the profound emotions that arise from it.

Szimpla Kert, Budapest's legendary ruin pub, will provide the perfect backdrop for this mesmerizing musical journey. Known for its eclectic atmosphere and vibrant energy, Szimpla Kert is a hub for art, music, and entertainment in the heart of the city.

Don't miss the opportunity to experience First Came The Shadow live at Szimpla Kert. Prepare to be captivated by their spellbinding melodies and transported to a world where emotions run deep. Stay tuned for the announcement of the date and time for this unforgettable event.

 ### PeaceBox - Peace up! No war anywhere!

Join us for PeaceBox - Peace up! No war anywhere! on Tuesday, October 24, 2023, from 5:30 PM to 8:00 PM UTC+02. The event is organized by PeaceBox and will feature a series of workshops aimed at promoting internal peace and exploring the meaning of peace in the world. These workshops will take place in Budapest from the middle of October to the middle of November.

The main focus of the workshops will be on peace, which is crucial for our mental well-being. Through various creative mediums such as visual art, moving image, photography, music, theater, and installation, participants will have the opportunity to express themselves and delve into the concept of peace. 

The workshops will emphasize the importance of accepting the rights of others, fostering good relationships with neighbors, and promoting the free flow of information. Together, we will seek answers to questions like, "What can we do for peace?"

One of the unique aspects of these workshops is the creative approach, which encourages teamwork, sharing personal stories, and building deeper connections with others. By participating, you will not only engage with your own creativity but also connect with like-minded individuals who are passionate about peace.

The workshops will also provide a platform for physical activity, learning new skills, giving back to others, and practicing mindfulness. It is not mandatory to attend all workshops, and the participation is free of charge.

The address for the event is Repair Cafe - Közösségi szerelde, Budapest 1085, Teleki László tér 7.

Here are the dates for the workshops:

- October 24, 2023, at 17:30
- October 31, 2023, at 17:30
- November 7, 2023, at 17:30
- November 14, 2023, at 17:30

The workshops will be conducted by Eranyak Oganova, also known as Achik. You can connect with Eranyak Oganova on Facebook at [https://www.facebook.com/eranyak.oganova](https://www.facebook.com/eranyak.oganova). 

The working language for the event is English, and we hope to see you there!

 ### 📅 EXTENDED MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION 📅

📅 EXTENDED MEMBERSHIP APPLICATION 📅

Are you interested in the field of public speaking, debating, and heated conversations? Have you been struggling to find people who share your passion regarding controversial yet essential topics of humanity? Do you wish to become a confident speaker and represent your university at international debating tournaments? If you answered YES to any of these questions, Budapest Debate Union is waiting for YOUR application!

Budapest Debate Union (BDU) is an English-speaking student organization that holds weekly debate meetups open to all students. This semester, the meetups take place every Monday and Wednesday from 7 pm. The events consist of three parts:

1. Workshop: Led by senior members of the society who have experience from international debate tournaments, the workshop aims to provide valuable insights and guidance on various aspects of debating.

2. Formal Debate: Participants engage in formal debates on specific motions introduced on the spot. This part of the meetup allows you to test and improve your complex debating skills, such as English public speaking, critical thinking, and being confident in any situation.

3. Feedback and Learning: Judges provide constructive feedback after every debate, allowing participants to progress on their learning curve in Corvinus Debate Society. This feedback helps refine your debating skills and enhances your ability to articulate your thoughts effectively.

At BDU, we believe that connections and friendships can serve as a reliable basis for enjoying university studies most efficiently. Therefore, we are working hard to set up an inclusive community that welcomes everyone regardless of gender, race, or religion.

If you want to get to know us more and become part of our community, don't forget to visit our next Debate Meetup. Whether you are a seasoned debater or just starting out, we welcome all levels of experience.

The deadline for submitting your membership application is October 29, 11 PM. Don't miss this opportunity to join a community of passionate debaters. Apply now at bit.ly/debate-application-a23. We can't wait to review your application and potentially welcome you to the Budapest Debate Union!
