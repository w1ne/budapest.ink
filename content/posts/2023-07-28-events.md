 ### godfater. x KOBUCI KERT x Július 28.

Event Name: godfater. x KOBUCI KERT x Július 28.
Location: 47.540747, 19.046219

Are you ready for a night of electrifying music? Don't miss the upcoming event "godfater. x KOBUCI KERT x Július 28." happening at Kobuci Kert, Budapest. This event will showcase the powerful and energetic performance of the supergroup, godfater., on July 28. 

Formed just over a year ago, godfater. has quickly established themselves as a force to be reckoned with in the music scene. Tátrai Tibor, Borlai Gergő, Szebényi Dániel, Gotthárd Mihály, and Kéri Sámuel make up this incredible quintet, and they are known for their dynamic and captivating live shows.

The band gained widespread popularity after their debut concert, which was sold out in less than 48 hours at the Müpa concert hall. Following their successful performance at the Akvárium Nagyhall, the band embarked on a series of sold-out concerts throughout the country.

Bringing their critically acclaimed and award-winning debut album "godfater. 0" to life on stage, the band will perform the songs without any modifications. Alongside their original compositions, the band will also showcase some iconic tracks from Tátrai Tibor's extensive repertoire, including the high-energy hit "Álljatok meg" by Skorpió, and the beautiful Hungarian ballad "Ne szeress" from Deák Bill Gyula's first album.

Tátrai Tibor, a highly acclaimed musician honored with the Kossuth and Liszt Awards, has been a key member of bands like Tátrai Band, Generál, Skorpió, Hobo Blues Band, Boom Boom, and Magyar Atom. Borlai Gergő is a world-renowned drummer who has played on over 500 albums with both local and international musicians. Szebényi Dani, an exceptional singer and keyboard player, has contributed to over 200 recordings and his songs have been streamed by more than 100 million people. Gotthárd Mihály, a guitar virtuoso, has played in various formations, including Electric Shock, alongside Borlai Gergő and Szebényi Dani. Kéri Samu, the youngest member of the group, is the first and only Hungarian bass guitarist to have studied at Berklee College of Music.

The band's live concerts are further enhanced by the vocal talents of Hegedűs Bori and Miviana, who add a captivating dimension to the sound and stage presence.

Don't miss the opportunity to witness the musical brilliance of godfater. as they take the stage at Kobuci Kert on July 28. Tickets are available in advance at a discounted rate, so be sure to secure yours early. This is a concert that promises to deliver an unforgettable experience filled with energy and passion. Grab your tickets now and get ready to be blown away by godfater.'s incredible performance!

 ### Fehér kesztyűs megemlékezés Andy Fletcherről

Join us for the Fehér kesztyűs megemlékezés Andy Fletcherről (White Gloved Tribute to Andy Fletcher) event in Budapest, Hungary. This special commemoration will take place during the Depeche Mode, Memento Mori Tour concert on Friday, July 28, 2023, at 9:00 PM UTC+02.

Andy Fletcher, a former member of Depeche Mode, sadly passed away in May of the previous year. To honor his memory, the current band members, Dave and Martin, will pay tribute to their former bandmate and friend during their performance of "World In My Eyes." As they reminisce, we invite all attendees to join us in expressing our condolences by raising our hands in a white-gloved salute, a symbol associated with Andy.

Furthermore, during the song "Never Let Me Down Again," we will recreate the iconic white-gloved waving that took place during the 2017 concert at Groupama Aréna in Budapest. It will be a powerful moment of unity and remembrance.

We invite all devoted Depeche Mode fans, as well as those who wish to pay their respects to Andy Fletcher's legacy, to join us for this heartfelt event. Let's come together and celebrate the music and memories of a beloved musician. Don't miss out on this unique opportunity to be part of the Fehér kesztyűs megemlékezés Andy Fletcherről in Budapest.
