 ### godfater. x KOBUCI KERT x Július 28.

**Event Announcement: godfater. x KOBUCI KERT x Július 28**

Get ready for an unforgettable night of music as godfater. takes over the stage at Kobuci Kert on July 28. This supergroup, formed just over a year ago, will be showcasing their latest songs and bringing the high energy and momentum that the band is known for. 

Composed of Tátrai Tibor, Borlai Gergő, Szebényi Dániel, Gotthárd Mihály, and Kéri Sámuel, godfater. has already gained a strong following. After their debut concert last June, they have performed several sold-out shows, including one at the prestigious Müpa concert hall where all tickets were snapped up within 48 hours. They also embarked on a successful tour across various cities in Hungary, filling every venue they played.

godfater.'s first album, "godfater. 0", which won the prestigious Fonogram award, was recorded live without any corrections. Alongside their own original compositions, the band chose songs from the extensive repertoire of Tátrai Tibor. Audiences can expect to hear explosive tracks like "Álljatok meg" by Skorpió and the beautiful Hungarian ballad "Ne szeress" from Deák Bill Gyula's debut album.

Tátrai Tibor, a recipient of the Kossuth and Liszt awards, and an Honored Artist, is a defining member not only of the Tátrai Band but also of iconic bands like Generál, Skorpió, Hobo Blues Band, Boom Boom, and Magyar Atom. Borlai Gergő, on the other hand, is on the path to becoming a world-renowned drummer, having played on nearly 500 albums with notable musicians from Hungary and abroad. The younger generation is represented by top talents, with the talented singer-keyboardist Szebényi Dani known for his exceptional vocals and being featured on over 200 recordings as a contributor and songwriter. Guitar virtuoso Gotthárd Mihály has also been part of numerous formations, including Electric Shock with Borlai Gergő and Szebényi Dani. Kéri Samu, the youngest member of the group and the first and only Hungarian bass guitarist at Berklee College of Music, is making his mark with his excellent playing fitting in perfectly with the esteemed lineup. 

Live shows are further enhanced by the incredible vocal talents of Bori Hegedűs and Miviana, who add an extra layer to both the sound and the stage presence.

Make sure to mark your calendars for this exclusive godfater. concert at Kobuci Kert on July 28. Early bird tickets are priced at 5000 Ft, advance tickets at 5500 Ft, and tickets on the day of the concert at 6000 Ft. Don't miss out on this incredible opportunity to witness the power and magic of godfater. live on stage. Join us for a special event in Budapest, Hungary, as we pay tribute to the late Andy Fletcher from Depeche Mode. Taking place on Friday, July 28, 2023, at 9:00 PM UTC+02, this event, titled "Fehér kesztyűs megemlékezés Andy Fletcherről," is organized by Zsolt Sipos.

Come and be part of this nostalgic and heartfelt event, celebrating the legacy of Andy Fletcher. Let us unite in remembering a cherished musician and paying our respects to the band. Join us in Budapest as we honor Andy Fletcher's memory with the white-gloved wave.
